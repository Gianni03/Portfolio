About me

Hello, my name is Gianni, I'm from Argentina and I'm a web developer. At the time I graduated from high school I was in doubt about what career I should do, computer systems or Music. The emotional won the battle and I went ahead and began to study music composition at the UNR. After some difficulties at work increased by the pandemic, a possibility of formation in web development came up, and without hesitation I started this other passion.

With my musical artistic background, I am always approached in a creative way, in the balance of the sensitive and the functional. Habituated to think from different perspectives, with lateral thinking or "outiside the box". By keeping the capacity of surprise intact, I enjoy observing how a solution is achieved through several paths and how the same path has different outcomes.

Perhaps words are not my best, but I do have an overwhelming need to express myself and show myself to the world, from the sensitive to the intelligent.
